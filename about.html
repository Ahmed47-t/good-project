Our Story

Let’s Change How We Manage Business

BENRO INDUSTRIES is a vibrant and innovative company at the forefront of the HVAC&R sector. We take pride in crafting premium quality air conditioning connecting tubes, a versatile range of insulated refrigeration copper and aluminium tubes, catering to both industrial and residential applications.

Furthermore, BENRO INDUSTRIES excels in producing polyethylene foam tubes for insulating ACR tubes and many other industrial applications.

Milestones

From a Ghardaia workshop to a leading Algerian HVAC manufacturer

Key moments along our journey of growth, investment and product diversification.

2019
  • Company established in Ghardaïa, Algeria.
2021
  • Relocation of the factory to the Industrial Zone.
  • Launch of the PE Extrusion line.
  • Launch of the 2nd Copper Cutting line.
2022
  • Launch of Aluminium welded tube production.
2023
  • Products homologated by 2 major A/C manufacturers.
  • Manpower oversees the 30.
  • Implementation of QMS.
2024
  • 2nd extension to the new plant.
  • Launch of the 2nd PE Extrusion line.
  • Manufactory management by ERP.
