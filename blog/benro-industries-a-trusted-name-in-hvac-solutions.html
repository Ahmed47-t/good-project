Benro Industries: A Trusted Name in HVAC Solutions

BENRO INDUSTRIES·April 12, 2025

Why Choose Benro Industries for Your HVAC Needs?

Benro Industries has established itself as a leader in the HVAC industry, offering high-quality air conditioning and refrigeration components. Based in Algeria, the company is committed to providing durable and efficient solutions tailored to both industrial and residential applications.

What Sets Benro Industries Apart?

  • Premium Materials: copper and aluminium tubing with superior insulation.
  • Compliance with International Standards: products adhere to EN 12735 specifications.
  • Efficient Performance: reduced BTU losses for better energy savings.
  • Global Reach: serving HVAC manufacturers, distributors and installers worldwide.

EN 12735 Standard and Its Importance

The EN 12735 standard ensures that refrigeration copper tubes meet stringent quality and safety requirements. Compliance with this standard guarantees:

  • Optimal wall thickness for pressure resistance.
  • Corrosion resistance for extended lifespan.
  • Compatibility with modern refrigerants.

Why Benro Industries is the Right Choice for HVAC Professionals

Comprehensive Product Range

Benro Industries offers a variety of HVAC solutions, including:

  • Single & Twin Insulated Copper Tubes: designed for enhanced performance.
  • Aluminium Tubes: lightweight yet durable alternatives to copper.
  • Polyethylene Tubes: excellent thermal insulation properties.

Industry Authority and Reliability

For further insights, refer to trusted industry sources such as ASHRAE (American Society of Heating, Refrigerating, and Air-Conditioning Engineers) and the International Copper Association.

By choosing Benro Industries, you are investing in high-quality, energy-efficient and internationally compliant HVAC components that ensure long-term savings and optimal performance.
